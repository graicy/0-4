Instructions: how to start the day right?
Little joys that will help you wake up faster and spend the day with a smile.
The beginning of the day is not his most pleasant moment. Few people wake up, like in the movies, happy and rested, awakened by a sunbeam. Most people associate morning with fatigue, traffic jams, crowding on public transport, a cup of coffee for breakfast and a lack of desire to do anything. And some even wake up already tired, without mood and any motivation. Setting up for a productive day like this isn't easy. How can you change your morning routine so that it suppresses fatigue and bad moods and provides energy for the whole day? Perhaps these simple but enjoyable rituals will help.

Drink a glass of water

Heard about it hundreds of times but never started? So it's time. After sleep, the body is often dehydrated, and a glass of water helps to start many processes, for example, improves metabolism. Soon, you will not notice that you are sorely thirsty after getting out of bed.

Do your exercises

If you have a little time in the morning, you can add sports to your schedule. If you want to tune in to a new day, catch harmony and sort out all thoughts on the shelves - meditation or yoga will do. Use YouTube tutorial videos or a relaxing music playlist. And if you need a charge of vivacity, you should choose a more active charge. It disperses the blood and helps to wake up completely.

Replace coffee matches

The easiest way to refresh your morning routine is to try a new breakfast drink. Enter there are not only black and green tea, but also yellow, red and even white. You may be a fan of matches, but don't know about it yet.

New emotions and impressions will definitely cheer you up. Besides, matcha is much healthier than coffee. The L-theanine contained in it slows down the absorption of caffeine and allows you to achieve an energetic effect without a sharp rise in pressure. Also, the drink has antioxidant properties.

Plan your day in advance

Think about what you will be doing during the day, what goals await you. Perhaps working deadlines and routine tasks will not be particularly pleasing. On the other hand, meeting friends, going to the cinema, theater and museums will help recharge your motivation and improve your mood.

Eat a healthy breakfast

An important part of the morning is breakfast. Don't eat on the run. Sit down calmly and pay attention to your food.

Remember that food should not only be tasty, but also visually beautiful. Then you will enjoy even simple sandwiches or oatmeal more.
