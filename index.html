Lazy Oatmeal with Chia Seeds
Lazy oatmeal is the easiest oatmeal porridge recipe that is prepared without boiling.
Lazy oatmeal is the easiest oatmeal porridge recipe that is prepared without boiling. The oatmeal is poured over with milk and left in the refrigerator overnight. In the morning you will have a delicious and healthy breakfast ready. With the advent of superfoods, the traditional lazy oatmeal recipe has expanded to include healthy chia. Thanks to the seeds, the texture of the porridge becomes like a pudding. For greater benefit, add peanut butter, it will turn out very satisfying. The dish can be served with berries or fruits.

The Oatmeal Oatmeal Recipe for the Night is a great healthy breakfast option that can be made several days in advance. It will appeal to those who do not want to cook in the morning. You just need to make preparations on Sunday evening for the entire working week. In the morning, you just need to stir the porridge in a jar and breakfast is ready.

Lazy Oatmeal Ingredients are served in two servings. In cans with a volume of 250-300 ml. Thanks to chia seeds and peanut butter, the porridge is very satisfying. Therefore, a small volume of cans is enough to have a full breakfast. Of course, there are some nuances that you should pay attention to:
  • Porridge is prepared with milk, water or drinking yoghurt (natural, no additives). If you are using milk, it is better to choose whole milk with at least 2.5% fat content. Or any vegetable milk - coconut, almond, oatmeal or cashew milk.
  • Chia seeds are optional. But with them, the texture of the oatmeal becomes like a pudding.
  • Peanut butter is also optional. But it makes you satiated. Plus, it has a very beneficial effect on the body.
  • Don't forget the sweetener. Oatmeal can be made with any sweetener you like: sugar, maple syrup, or honey.
  • Various fruit additives are used. From dried fruits to any seasonal berries and fruits. And also the dish can be supplemented with flax seeds, nuts and spices (cinnamon, nutmeg, cardamom).
Ingredients:
  • A glass of coconut milk (I have homemade + coconut cream). Any vegetable or whole can be used.
  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 2-3 tablespoons of honey or syrup;
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter;
  • Fruit for decoration - peach, banana, etc.
Lazy oatmeal with chia and peanut butter is ready. Stir before serving, you can put on a plate or eat from a can. Decorate with berries or fruits. Enjoy your meal!
Porridge in a jar can be stored for up to 5 days in the refrigerator. It is very convenient to make such blanks for several days at once. In the morning, you don't need to cook the oatmeal, just stir it and a delicious, healthy breakfast is ready.
